Briony Conway

Youth and Community Worker at Ballysally Presbyterian Church

I live in Coleraine on the beautiful North Coast of Northern Ireland with my husband Matías and our cat Púa. We swapped the sunny streets of Santiago, Chile, for the rainy beaches of NI in 2020, arriving here on the fateful lockdown day of 23rd March! I am a youth and community worker for the Presbyterian Church in the estate of Ballysally, where I run the weekly youth, children, and women's events, visit families in the community, and work in the local schools. In my free time you can probably find me drinking a hot chocolate, going for a run, watching a movie, or out paddleboarding.
